Instructions

Work in a small group. Read each case study, discuss the questions, and type your group answers into the text boxes.

Complete all three case studies on this page, then select Submit Group Responses.

Case Study 1: The Free Game Coins

Maya enjoys playing an online game with classmates. While she is playing, a player she does not know sends her a private message.

“Hi Maya! I can give you free game coins. Click this website and log in with your game username and password. Don’t tell anyone because this is a secret offer.”

Case Study 2: The Class Collaboration File

A class is working on a shared online document for a school project. One student accidentally posts a screenshot that shows another student’s full name, email address, and a private comment from the teacher.

Case Study 3: The New Homework App

Noah finds a website that says it can help organise homework. Before he can use it, the website asks for his full name, date of birth, school name, home address, phone number, and permission to send notifications.
